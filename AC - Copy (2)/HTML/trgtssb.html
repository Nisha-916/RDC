What is SSB?

SSB (Service Selection Board) is an organization that assesses the candidates for becoming officers in the Indian Defence Forces. The board evaluates the suitability of the candidate for becoming an officer using a standardized protocol of eveluation system which constitutes personality,Intelligence tests and Interviews. SSB selection procedure is based on three mantras : manas(mind), Vacha(speech), and karmana(action)

The process of SSB is designed by DRDO's Defence Institute of Psychological Research(DIPR) lab situated in Delhi.

There are no right or wrong answers in SSB as every individual acts in a different way to the same situation.The aim is to check whether the candidate is the right fit to become an officer in the defence forces or not.

SSB Stages

  • Stage 1: Screening Test (OIR Test + Picture Perception and Discussion Test)
  • Stage 2: Psychological Tests (TAT, WAT, SRT, SD)
  • Stage 3: Group Testing Officer (GTO) Tasks
  • Stage 4: Interview
  • Stage 5: Conference

Preparation Tips

Here are some key tips to help you prepare for the SSB interview:

  • Enhance your communication skills and confidence.
  • Stay updated on current affairs and defense-related topics.
  • Practice psychological tests like TAT, WAT, SRT, and SD.
  • Participate in group discussions and leadership exercises.
